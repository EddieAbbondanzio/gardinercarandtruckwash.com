Who We Are

Gardiner Car And Truck Wash offers a new state of the art Touch-Free Automatic Wash (installed 2021), 3 Self Serve Auto Bays and 1 Self Serve Industrial Truck Bay (accommodates commercial trucks, buses, campers, boats, recreational vehicles and even 18 wheelers). Self Serve Vacuums (includes a spot remover/rug cleaner and a Fragrance spray option), Car Care Product Vending Machine, Change Machine and customer complimentary towel station and air dryer that is also motorcycle friendly.
