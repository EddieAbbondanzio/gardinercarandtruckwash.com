Self Serve Bays

Each of our 4 self serve bays are equipped with a high pressure wash wand and foaming wash brush. For convenience all bays accept cash or credit cards.

Our Self Serve Industrial Truck bay has a height clearance of 14 feet and can easily accommodate the largest of trucks, buses, campers and even 18 wheelers. Our large parking lot provides easy access for all trucks.

Truck Bay is self service for washing exterior of vehicles only. We do not offer Wash-Out Services.

Self Serve Dog Wash

Due to freezing temperatures the dog wash is only available from May to September.

Washing your dog can be challenging at home so we provide everything you need with our new do it yourself dog wash station that accommodates all breeds and sizes. This is available to you 24 hours, 7 days a week and no appointment is necessary.

Our easy get in and out elevated dog wash station has a latched door, non-slip mat and tie-out to keep your furry friend safely contained. Comfortable warm water, high quality all natural oatmeal shampoo, flea and tick shampoo, skunk odor remover, conditioner and a high and low blow dryer.

Vacuums

High-powered self-service vacuums are located adjacent to the building. All vacuums accept credit cards, cash or coin operated and a change machine is available on site. They are a great value for those looking to clean the interior of their vehicle.
