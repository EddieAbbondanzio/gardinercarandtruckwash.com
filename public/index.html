Who We Are

Gardiner Car And Truck Wash is an eco friendly touch-free autowash, 3 self serve car bays and 1 self serve industrial truck bay (accommodates trucks, buses, campers and even 18 wheelers). Self serve vacuums, car care product vending machine, change machine and customer complimentary towel and window washing station, along with a motorcycle friendly station stocked with an air dryer and towels.
